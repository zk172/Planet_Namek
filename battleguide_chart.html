An Introduction:
The chart below documents every last one of the 187 battles that took place in the Dragon Ball manga. Actually, this list contains more than 187 battles. Why, you ask? Because elsewhere in Daizenshuu #2 (the Daizenshuu which contains the "187 battles" list, from which this battle information was collected), the book outlines other battles which the list of "187 battles" omits. In these cases, we have numbered the omitted battles by number of the next battle or the preceding battle (whichever is more appropriate), followed by a letter (i.e. 108a, 108b). Why didn't we just stick to what everybody considers to be common knowledge? Because we feel we owe it to the fans not to just translate a list out of some book, but to verify the information, no matter how authoritative or official the source is. Once again, we debunk a commonly held fact as mere myth. In addition, this chart now document all of the battles which were exclusive to the TV Series, Movies, and TV Specials.

How This Chart Works:
All battles divided into their respective sagas. Manga battles are listed under the manga volume and chapter in which they began (NOT when they finished). TV Series Battles are sorted by the episode number in which they began. If a battle ended in a different episode than it began, then it is noted. Follow the key above each table for more specifics.
